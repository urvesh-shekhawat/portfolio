About Me

Philosophy & Expertise

I am a Senior Web Developer with a strong focus on digital inclusion. With over seven years of experience building user-centric interfaces, I specialize in bringing applications in line with WCAG 2.2 AA and AAA criteria.

I believe that web applications must be beautiful, performance-driven, and accessible to everyone. By writing clean, semantic HTML and prioritizing keyboard accessibility, I help companies lower legal risk, improve SEO, and expand their user base.
